# Energy Audit

Focused workflow for auditing a building's energy performance.

## Steps

1. **file-index** — scan and index all documents in the project folder
2. **pdf-extract** — extract structured data from utility bills and energy reports
3. **audette-energy-data** — validate and compile energy consumption data
4. **system-details** — collect detailed building systems information

After each step, pause and confirm before proceeding.
